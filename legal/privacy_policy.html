Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

This Privacy & Cookie Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Galaxy websites and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in compliance with applicable international and national data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve our services:

Data Category Examples of Data Collected
1.1. Account Data Details you provide when registering, such as your username, email address, and profile preferences.
1.2. Transaction Data Payment details (processed securely by third-party providers), shipping address, phone number, and purchase history.
1.3. User-Generated Content (UGC) Any content you voluntarily post or upload, including messages in the Community Chat, comments, posts, and submissions.
1.4. Technical & Usage Data Information about your device (browser type, operating system), IP address, general geographic location, and website usage patterns (via analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To provide, personalize, and maintain our services (e.g., loading your profile).
  • To process your orders, complete transactions, and deliver merchandise.
  • To communicate with you about community updates, events, or promotions.
  • To monitor and moderate User-Generated Content for compliance with our Terms of Use (e.g., checking for harassment, hate speech, or spam).

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and track site performance.

Necessary/Functional Cookies

Essential for core site functionality (e.g., login, security).

Performance/Analytics Cookies

Used to collect non-personal data on how users interact with the site, helping us improve performance.

Your Consent:

By continuing to use our Services, you consent to the placement of Necessary Cookies. For Performance/Analytics Cookies, you can manage or disable them in your browser settings.

4. Data Processing Location and International Transfers

  • Data Location: Our primary data processing and storage servers are located outside of the user's country of residence, primarily in the United States (US), utilizing Google's Firebase services.
  • International Transfers: When you use our Services, your data is transferred to and processed in the US. We take the following measures to ensure adequate protection of data transferred from regions like the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK:
    • We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other applicable legal mechanisms provided by Google in their Data Processing Terms to ensure your data receives protection equivalent to EU standards.
    • By using our Services, you consent to the transfer of your personal data to the US for the purposes outlined in this policy.

5. Third-Party Services and Data Processors

We use trusted third-party providers who act as Data Processors on our behalf. These include:

  • Google Firebase/Cloud Platform: For hosting, database (Firestore), and backend services.
  • Payment Processors: To securely handle financial transactions.
  • Shipping Carriers: To fulfill and track order delivery.
  • Google Analytics: For site performance tracking.

These third parties process your data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification and Erasure: Ask us to correct or delete your personal data (the "Right to be Forgotten"), subject to legal obligations.
  • Portability: Where technically feasible, request your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Request the restriction of processing your personal data in certain circumstances.

You may exercise these rights by contacting us at legal@teamgalaxy.dev.

7. Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures (like encryption and secure protocols) to protect your data.
  • However, no online system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
