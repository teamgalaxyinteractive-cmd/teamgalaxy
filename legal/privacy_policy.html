Last Updated: April 2026

Galaxy Designs operates premium design and digital asset services. For the purposes of data protection laws, Galaxy Designs is the data controller of the personal data collected through our Services.

Email: info@teamgalaxy.net

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and personalize our design services and platform.
  • To process invoices, provide purchased assets, and deliver digital files.
  • To communicate with you regarding project updates, revisions, or important notices.
  • To improve and optimize our Services, including debugging and performance monitoring.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where data protection law (such as the GDPR in the EEA/UK) applies, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate Interests: Such as improving Services, securing our systems, and running analytics.

  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful requests.
